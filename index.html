Data Privacy & Handling

Your privacy is paramount. We are committed to transparency and minimizing data collection.

  • What We Store: The only personal information we store on our servers is the authentication data required for you to log in automatically on future visits.
  • What We Don't Store: All user-generated content, including code analysis, images, chats, and other creations, is never stored on our servers.

You have full control over your creations. Data can be saved via explicit confirmation to:

  • Your personal Google Drive account.
  • Your local device storage.

If you do not explicitly save your work, it will be stored temporarily for your current session only. This data will be permanently lost if the application is closed, restarted, or uninstalled.
